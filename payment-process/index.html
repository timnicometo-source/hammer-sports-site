Hammer Sports Officials

Payment Process

Hammer Sports strives to pay officials on a timely basis. Heidi will invoice schools and leagues every Sunday evening for the previous week's games, Monday through Sunday. The first day of the weekly pay period is Monday, and the last day is the following Sunday.

Heidi pays officials by Sunday evening at midnight. Please do not ask for payment earlier or for an exact time, as it will vary each week depending on her work schedule. Thanks!

You have three options to get paid: debit card, ACH, or mailed check. Please review the detailed information below and choose only one option. If you are new to Hammer Sports, Heidi will reach out to you when she is ready to pay you for your first games and ask which option you prefer.

If you are already receiving payments, you do not have to share your option again unless you change accounts or payment methods.

1. Debit Card

Debit card payments are instant. If you have a debit card, when Heidi processes funds to you, you will receive a text asking you to accept funds from our bank, United Community. You will enter your debit card number safely and securely. Once entered, funds go directly to your account.

The majority of officials use this method. You only need to enter your debit card once, and future payments will automatically go to that account unless you change debit cards and let Heidi know.

Important: You are responsible for entering your correct debit card information. We cannot see it or enter it for you. Once payment has been processed and leaves our account, it will go to whatever card number you entered.

If you make an error and the funds go somewhere else, we will not pay you again for that payment. Please double-check the card number before accepting payment.

2. Checking Account / ACH

ACH payments require you to send your account and routing number to Heidi by text, email, or mailed voided check. ACH payments typically take 2–3 days to arrive in your checking account once funds are processed.

If you are not comfortable sharing your banking information, you will need to choose debit card or mailed check instead. We do not share your information. Heidi enters it into our bank system, and it is only used when processing your payment.

3. Mailed Check

A mailed hard check, also known as bill pay, takes approximately 7–10 days from when funds are processed. Checks will be mailed to the address listed on your Arbiter profile.

We do not use Arbiter Pay, Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, or any other payment options except the three choices listed above.
