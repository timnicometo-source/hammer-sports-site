For Officials

Current & Prospective Officials

Becoming a Hammer Sports Official

How do I become an official with Hammer Sports?

The first step is to fill out our online application form. Once you have applied and we have confirmed your registration, we will get you started with our scheduling system.

Become an Official
Do I need experience to join?

Not at all. We welcome new officials and believe everyone has to start somewhere. We provide resources and can help connect you with mentors so you feel confident and prepared on the court or field.

What sports are you currently looking for officials in?

We are always looking for dedicated officials for all the sports we staff, including basketball, volleyball, slow-pitch and fast-pitch softball, baseball, soccer, and football/flag football.

Working & Getting Paid

How do I get assigned to games?

We use Arbiter Sports, an online scheduling system where you can set your availability, view open games, and accept your assignments. This makes it easy to build a schedule that works for you.

We send out mass Arbiter open-game emails regularly. Please use the Hammer Sports contact page for any games you are interested in and qualified to work. This helps keep requests organized and routed properly.

Please be patient and do not expect the world right away. We have a lot of games, but a lot of officials as well. If you are reliable, work hard, display a good attitude, follow protocol, and stay on top of the rules, you will have spots working with Hammer Sports.

How much can I expect to be paid?

We offer competitive game fees that are based on the sport, level of play, and length of the contest. Our officials are paid on a regular and reliable schedule.

For more information, review our Payment Process.

Do I need to buy my own uniform and equipment?

Yes, officials are responsible for their own gear. For a full breakdown of what you will need, check out our Uniform & Equipment Guide.

For first-time officials, we strongly recommend visiting Dave’s Sports Shop in Maple Grove . Their staff is great at helping you get the right fit for essential gear like masks, chest protectors, pants, shirts, and shoes.

Hammer Sports official merch: If you are also looking for unique, custom referee and umpire clothing, Hammer Sports has a variety of items, including shorts and polos you can wear while working games.

Support & Development

What happens if I have an issue at a game site?

If you run into any issues, whether it is a conflict, weather delay, or a problem with the facility, your first call should be to your Hammer Sports assigner. We are here to support you.

How can I improve and get assigned to higher-level games?

We encourage you to seek feedback from your partners and experienced officials. Let us know you are interested in advancing, and we will do our best to get you observed and provide opportunities for higher-level assignments as you develop your skills.

Schools & Organizations

Hiring Hammer Sports Officials

How do I request officials for our games, leagues, or tournaments?

Simply head to our Hire Officials page and fill out the contact form with details about your event or season. We will get back to you promptly to discuss your needs and provide a quote. The more advance notice you can give, the better we can serve you.

Hire Officials
What sports and geographic areas do you cover?

We provide qualified officials for a wide range of youth and amateur sports throughout the Twin Cities metro and Greater Minnesota. Contact us to see how we can cover your specific location.

What are your rates for providing officials?

Our pricing is competitive and transparent. Rates depend on the sport, level of competition, and number of officials required. Please contact us directly for a detailed quote tailored to your organization’s needs.

Quality & Reliability

Are your officials certified and properly trained?

Absolutely. We ensure our officials are registered with the MSHSL and other relevant governing bodies where applicable. They are trained on the latest rules and officiating mechanics to provide a fair and professionally managed game.

How do you handle last-minute cancellations or no-shows?

We have a large network of officials on standby. In the event of a last-minute emergency, we work quickly to find a qualified replacement to ensure your game is covered.

How do you handle feedback or concerns about an official?

We value your feedback. If you have any concerns or compliments regarding an official’s performance, please contact us directly. This helps us maintain the highest standards of quality for our services.
